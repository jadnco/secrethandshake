What is it?

Goodwork, eh?

Goodwork is a one day event where Secret Handshake volunteers devote a day of creative work to a local non-profit. Our first event was held on Saturday March 9, 2013, and we supported United Way Partner Agencies with our skills in branding, design, marketing strategy, social media strategy, writing and web development projects.
