Goodwork was Good!

What is it?

Goodwork, eh?

Goodwork is a one day event where Secret Handshake volunteers devote a day of creative work to a local non-profit. On Saturday March 9, 2013, we'll be supporting United Way Partner Agencies with our skills in branding, design, marketing strategy, social media strategy, writing and web development projects.

Click the button above to sign up now if you're interested in getting involved, or make a donation to the United Way where 100% of your contribution goes directly to great organizations like the ones listed below.
